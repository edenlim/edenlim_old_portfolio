Manpower tracking application

Summary

Requested by my unit Commanding Officer, I was tasked with the responsibility to create a manpower tracking application. Check out how the story unfolds from here on!

  • Inexperience

      • I started the project without any prior experience or knowledge in Google app script. Before this, I only have rudimentary background in Python.
      This hindered the progress of the development greatly as there was a lot of time wasted on trial and errors, researching, guessing and intuition, and a lot of digesting.

  • Lack of manpower

      • As the only member in the team, I was concurrently performing other duties for my unit. A lot of times, I would be working on the code and be interrupted. This would often break my train of thoughts and lengthen the duration of the development.

  • Security constraints and lack of accessibility

      • Due to security reasons, I was unable to download any files online. All of my code has to be written and compiled online. To get around this, I would leverage MIT App Inventor 2 and Google App Script for my project. Competition for a computer with internet accessibility was fierce in a military environment, and I found myself often halting my progress for others whom may need to use the computer. The situation was often exacerbated when relatively rude colleagues log off my account without informing me, where I would often lose progress.

  • Lack of clarity

      • Despite being tasked to develop the app, there was a lot of uncertainty when coming up with the idea of the app.
      As I was given no context, I was uncertain what was needed of me. Initially I was told to create a relatively simple application that requires, upon scanning, registration of an Id and associated time. Little did I realised, there would be much more issues...


Eventually, you have to get started. Sometimes in life, excuses are just not enough.

Desperation forges Creativity.

It was really difficult at the start. I was very lost and confused. But desperation can really push a man beyond his capabilities. The world is large, and there are plenty of experts with solutions out there. I just have to find my answers. All I need is a bit of time, perserverance and dedication.

The answers lies right in front of you

Thankfully for me, I live in an era where knowledge is rapidly accessible. The answer is always out there- and most of them are just quite literally just a press of a button away.

Acquiring knowledge is a form of imitation.

I often find myself looking through Youtube tutorials, looking for answers in stack overflow, or using javascript tutor to figure out my errors. Many times I would copy and paste other people's code and try to figure out how to change it to fit my needs.
Looking back, the ingredient to my accomplisment was simple. Positive outlook, determination and an inquisitive mind. The journey had taught me many useful and I was glad to have volunteered for the project.
